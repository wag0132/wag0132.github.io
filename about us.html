Our Story

Everything started with a very special woman — my Nonna. Her love for the kitchen was more than just cooking, it was how she showed love, care, and joy. Every meal she made carried tradition, warmth, and family. She's the root of everything we do at Benvenuti Pizzeria.

The passion Nonna passed down didn't stop there. My mother became the second most important person in my culinary journey. She taught me not only how to cook, but how to care — how food brings people together. She made sure our kitchen was a place of learning, love, and unforgettable flavors.

I started working with pizza when I was just 14. Over the years, I’ve had the privilege of working in incredible kitchens — from Savoy in West Hartford to Square Peg and beyond. Each place helped shape my vision, refine my craft, and fuel the dream of opening my own pizzeria.

Today, Benvenuti Pizzeria is the result of those memories, those teachings, and that family passion — passed from Nonna’s hands to mine.
